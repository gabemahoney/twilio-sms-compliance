Apiary SMS provides text message alerts about agent task completion, system notifications, and important updates.

By opting in to Apiary SMS, you agree to receive automated text messages at the mobile number you provided.

You opt-in by providing your phone number and explicit consent via the Apiary configuration process.

Message frequency varies depending on agent activity and system events. You may receive multiple messages per day during active periods.

Message and data rates may apply. Standard SMS charges from your mobile carrier will apply to all messages sent and received.
